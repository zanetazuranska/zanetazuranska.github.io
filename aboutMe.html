About Me

I am a 22-year-old Applied Computer Science student and a passionate gamer. I have been programming for around 10 years, which has shaped both my technical skills and my approach to game development. Coding has always been a natural part of my life rather than just a field of study.

I am currently specializing in Game Development and Computer Simulations. My main career goal is to focus on gameplay systems, particularly combat design, player abilities, enemy AI interactions, and responsive, engaging gameplay mechanics.

Education & Research

I am currently working on my engineering thesis in the field of Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning. The research focuses on agent behavior and interaction dynamics, using the "Hide and Seek" scenario as a case study. This allows me to explore emergent behaviors in simulated environments and AI-driven decision making.

Skills & Mindset

I learn new technologies quickly and enjoy working on challenging and unfamiliar problems. I value creativity, collaboration, and continuous improvement in both technical and design aspects of development.

I am actively developing my career in the game development industry and always open to new projects and opportunities that allow me to grow as a programmer and designer.
