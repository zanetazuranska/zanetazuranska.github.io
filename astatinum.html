Astatinum

Astatinum is a story-driven game created for the “Turniej Trójgamiczny” competition, where our project ranked in the top 10 teams.

The game tells the story of a young girl living in a post-war world, trying to save her ill grandfather. His heart is supported by a special device that requires astatinum — an extremely rare and hard-to-obtain element. Faced with this challenge, she sets out alone on a dangerous journey to find it.
