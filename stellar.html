Stellar Outbreak

Stellar Outbreak is a sci-fi action game created in collaboration with friends from Strong Boat Studio and developed in cooperation with Woodland Games.

The player takes on the role of a scientist and crew member of a space expedition sent to conduct a high-level experiment for the sake of the nation. However, the mission quickly spirals out of control when a mysterious virus is released onboard. Humans begin to mutate, and it becomes clear that returning to Earth was never part of the plan.

My Role in the Project

This project was developed as a full-day challenge within Strong Boat Studio. I was responsible for both gameplay systems and project coordination.

Gameplay & Systems:

  • Combat system
  • Mutant AI behavior
  • NPC system
  • Animation logic
  • Quick Time Events (QTE)
  • Weapon systems
  • Stealth / hiding system
  • Environmental trash system
  • Final boss implementation
  • Particles & VFX

Project Management:

I also coordinated the development process, managing tasks and communication within the team. Despite being a one-day challenge, it was a highly intensive experience that significantly improved my skills.
